Built for Meaningful Opportunities

What Makes a Referral Qualify?

The program is designed for meaningful service projects, including custom website development and other approved high-value creative services.

  • The prospect must be new to Echo Craft Studio.
  • The referral must be properly connected to the partner.
  • Echo Craft must approve and accept the project.
  • The client must complete payment for qualifying work.
  • Canceled, refunded, duplicate, or self-referrals do not qualify.

Frequently Asked Questions

Before You Apply

Does it cost anything to join?

No. Applying and participating are free. Every application is reviewed, and acceptance is not automatic.

Is every applicant approved?

No. Echo Craft reviews each submission for legitimacy, professionalism, fit, and the applicant’s intended referral approach.

When is compensation earned?

Compensation is earned after a properly tracked referral becomes a paying client and completes a qualifying project accepted by Echo Craft.

Does every Echo Craft service qualify?

No. The program focuses on approved higher-value services. Eligibility is confirmed before a referred project begins.

Can I refer myself?

No. Self-referrals, duplicate referrals, canceled projects, refunded projects, and improperly tracked referrals do not qualify.

How will I track my activity?

Approved partners receive a secure dashboard with their referral link, QR code, visits, activity, commissions, and payout history.
