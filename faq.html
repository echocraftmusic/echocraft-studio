Common Questions

Everything You Need to Know

Clear answers about working with Echo Craft, from the first conversation through final delivery.

01

What services does Echo Craft offer?

Echo Craft Creative Studio offers commercial production, website design, brand storytelling, video editing, creative consulting, and custom music and voiceovers.

02

How do I start a project?

Begin by contacting us through the website. We will learn about your goals, audience, timeline, and creative needs before recommending the right direction.

03

Do you work with clients outside Florida?

Yes. Many creative services can be completed remotely, allowing Echo Craft to work with businesses throughout the United States and beyond.

04

How long does a project take?

Timelines depend on the size and complexity of the project. After the initial consultation, you will receive a timeline based on your specific needs.

05

Are revisions included?

Revision terms are clearly outlined before the project begins. This keeps expectations, timelines, and deliverables clear for everyone.

06

Can I hire Echo Craft for one service?

Absolutely. You can request one focused service or combine several services into a larger creative solution based on your goals.

07

Do you create custom music and voiceovers?

Yes. Original music and professional voiceovers can be created for commercials, websites, promotional videos, podcasts, and campaigns.

08

Is a deposit required?

A deposit may be required to reserve production time and begin work. Payment requirements are explained clearly before project approval.
